Industry Context

Geo-based currency display is standard practice —
and Google says it's not cloaking

How competitors do it
Shopify GeoIP auto-display via Markets. On shared domains, geo content is not indexed — they accept the SEO tradeoff.
WooCommerce Plugins (WPML, Aelia, etc.) auto-switch via GeoIP. No prominent SEO disclosure to merchants. Schema mismatch is a known, unsolved issue.
BigCommerce Built-in geo-detection. Multi-currency with hreflang and canonical tags.
Amazon Uses card-issuing country for currency. Separate marketplace domains per region.
Google's stance

Changing price display based on geo is not cloaking — as long as core content stays the same. Google only crawls one version and won't see different prices.

What is cloaking: detecting bots and serving them different content.

Our approach

Canonical HTML in business currency, client-side JS swap for visitors. Standard pattern — aligned with how competitors and Google guidelines handle it.
